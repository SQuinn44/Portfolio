Portfolio

Engagements and Elopements

Shooting fun, adventurous engagement photos and elopements is a huge part of my business. From coast to coast, I have shot small, intimate weddings with beautiful natural backdrops. Be prepared to have an insanely goofy day out taking photos of you and your person being nerds. Get authentic photos of your connection.

Seniors and Models

One-on-one sessions are relaxed, comfortable and fun! Most people tell me they are 'awkward' in front of the camera. Well, that's where I come in. I am there to help capture your inner model AND your weirdo self. There is a massive value in taking photos that capture your personality inside and out.

Local Weddings and Events

Capturing the biggest day of your life is one of my greatest pleasures. I love to be there to capture those big 'I do' moments, and all the tiny moments in between. Handling large groups and wedding parties is a breeze! No need to stress out at all when I when I am there at the ready with my camera!

Travel and Architecture

Looking for someone to take photos of your favorite place? Want to purchase gorgeous prints from around the globe? I have you covered there too! Having traveled over seas to photograph tourist attractions, hotels, restaurants and more, I can cover your photographic travel needs! I can bring my camera and a backpack and be ready to shoot anywhere, rain or shine.
